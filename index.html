How it works
⬡

Recovery phrase identity

Your account is a 24-word recovery phrase. It derives your private key locally — nothing is sent to a server. Authentication is a cryptographic proof, not a password check.

◎

Multi-zone distribution

Accounts spread across independent zones worldwide. Your browser routes to the fastest healthy zone automatically — no central point of failure.

⬘

Open network

Run your own zone. Serve accounts, watches, or webhooks. Join or leave the network freely — no permission required.

◈

Anti-bot proof of work

Registration and login require a Hashcash-style proof of work. No CAPTCHA, no third party — the work is the gate.

⬙

Real-time watches

Monitor any EVM address for deposits, transfers, and contract events. Receive signed payloads to any webhook endpoint.

⬚

Built on a blockchain

Every watch, webhook, and event is a signed transaction on an open network. No central operator controls the ledger — anyone can run a node and join.
